Subways of Budapest

Game Rules

Objective

Build Budapest's metro system by connecting stations across the city to maximize your score.

How to Play

  • Drawing Sections: Click on a station and drag to another station to draw a metro line segment. Sections can be drawn at 90° or 45° angles following the grid.
  • Station Cards: Draw cards from the deck to determine which station you must connect to next.
  • Joker Card: The Joker allows you to connect to any available station on the board.
  • Rounds: Each round consists of drawing 8 cards. After 8 cards, a new metro line begins with a different color.

Building Rules

  • Segments cannot intersect with each other
  • Only one segment can connect any two stations
  • No loops allowed - you cannot revisit stations on the same metro line
  • Segments cannot pass through other stations

Scoring

Round Score: (Districts Covered × Max Stations in One District) + Danube Crossings

Final Score: Sum of Round Scores + Station Points + Junction Bonuses

  • 2 points for each station with 2 lines
  • 5 points for each station with 3 lines
  • 9 points for each station with 4 lines
